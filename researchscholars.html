Prof. Madhusudhan Singh

Prof. Madhusudhan Singh is a Professor in Department of Electrical Engineering, Delhi Technological University (DTU), formerly Delhi College of Engineering (DCE), Delhi. Dean Academic (UG), since Jun 2015. Member, Board of Management, since Aug 2016. Dean Student Welfare, from Feb 2013 to Dec 2014. Head of the Electrical Engineering Department, from Apr 2013 to May 2019. Member of Academic Council, DTU, since 2009. Chairman, Board of Studies (BoS), DTU, from 2013 to 2019. Member, Board of Studies (BoS), DTU, since 2009. Member, Board of Studies (BoS), NSUT, since 2019. Officer-in-Charge Examination Cell, from 2003 to 2009. Controller of Examination, from 2009 to 2013. Member of High Power Committee appointed by Govt. of NCT of Delhi, from 2011 to 2013. Associate Head of Electrical Engineering Department, from 2005 to 2010. Chief Warden, from 2006 to 2009. Procter, from 2009 to 2010. Member RDC/BoS, AKTU, Lucknow, since 2014. Chairman Scrutiny Committee CAS Promotions and Direct Recruitment of Teaching, since 2016. Chairman Admissions BE/BTech, from 2009 to 2011.

He is a coordinator of Center of Excellence(CoE) for Electronic Vehicles and Related Technologies(EVRT) at DTU.

He is also a member of various societies like:

  • Senior member IEEE (Institution of Electrical & Electronics Engineers), (M-85016850) USA.
  • Life Member of Indian Society for Technical Education (LM13231), New Delhi.
  • Fellow Member Institution of Engineer (India), Kolkata.
  • Branch Councellor, The IET (Institutions of Engineering and Technology) formerly IEE, UK, DTU student chapter, at Delhi Technological University, Bawana Road, Delhi-42.
  • Fellow Institution of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineers (IETE), New Delhi.
